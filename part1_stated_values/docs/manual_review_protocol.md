# Manual Review Protocol

## Page Candidate Review

Confirm that each candidate:

- belongs to the official parent company or documented historical domain
- primarily describes company identity, mission, purpose, or values
- is comparable with the selected page family across years
- is not a product, subsidiary, careers, newsroom, or campaign page

## Snapshot Review

Review every fallback, redirect, page-family transition, low-quality record, and automated flag. Confirm that:

- the capture is in the target year
- it is the closest eligible capture to June 30
- rejected closer captures have defensible reasons
- redirects preserve page identity
- adjacent-year captures were not used as substitutes

## Extraction Review

Compare replayed HTML with cleaned text. Mark whether:

- substantive content was retained
- navigation/footer/cookie boilerplate was removed
- meaningful wording was accidentally removed
- the result is coherent enough for analysis

## Change Review

Review examples from each change class. Confirm that changes are substantive rather than extraction noise, and that missing adjacent years produce null comparisons.

## Theme Review

For every reviewed theme:

- locate its evidence in the cleaned text
- confirm the evidence supports the category
- check salience and framing
- reject unsupported inferences

## Minimum Final Audit

- all unresolved or failed records
- all manual overrides and page replacements
- all low-confidence records
- at least one row per company
- at least 10% of usable rows
- twenty full provenance traces from final row to Wayback replay
